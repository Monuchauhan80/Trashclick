Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I report waste in my area?

You can report waste by navigating to the "Report Waste" page, filling out the form with details about the waste location, type, and quantity, and submitting it along with optional photos. Our team will verify the report and coordinate with local authorities for cleanup.

How do I sell my recyclable materials?

To sell recyclable materials, go to the "Sell Waste" page and create a listing with details about your recyclables, including type, quantity, and condition. You can set your own price or accept offers from recyclers in your area. TrashClick facilitates the connection, but the transaction is between you and the buyer.

How do I earn points on TrashClick?

You can earn points by reporting waste, selling recyclables, donating food, participating in community cleanup events, and inviting friends to join the platform. Points can be redeemed for rewards, discounts with partner businesses, or donated to environmental causes.

How can I join a community cleanup event?

Community cleanup events are listed on the "Community" page. You can browse upcoming events in your area, view details about each event, and register to participate. You can also create your own event and invite others to join.
