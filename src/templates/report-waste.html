Report Waste

Help keep your community clean by reporting waste. Your reports will help organize cleanup efforts and track environmental progress.

Or click directly on the map to set a location

Reporting Guidelines

  • Be as specific as possible about the waste location
  • Include a photo when possible to help cleanup teams identify the waste
  • Rate the severity honestly to help prioritize cleanup efforts
  • Make sure you're reporting in a public area or land you have permission to access
  • Don't put yourself in danger to report waste
  • For hazardous waste, contact local authorities if immediate action is needed
