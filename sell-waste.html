How It Works

1

Create Your Listing

Fill out the form with details about your recyclable materials, including type, quantity, condition, and photos.

2

Receive Offers

Local recyclers and waste collectors will contact you with offers to purchase your materials.

3

Arrange Pickup

Once you accept an offer, you can arrange a pickup time that works for both parties.

4

Get Paid & Earn Points

Receive payment for your materials and earn TrashClick points for your contribution to recycling.

Tips for Selling

  • Clean materials typically sell for higher prices
  • Sorting materials by type makes them more valuable
  • Clear, well-lit photos increase your chances of selling
  • Be specific about quantities and conditions
  • Respond promptly to inquiries and offers
