Food Donation Tips

  • Ensure all food is properly stored and still safe to eat
  • Include clear preparation/use-by dates for homemade foods
  • List all ingredients and potential allergens
  • Provide clear storage and reheating instructions
  • Take clear photos that accurately show the food
