About TrashClick

Transforming waste management through community engagement and technology

Our Mission

At TrashClick, our mission is to create a cleaner, greener future by revolutionizing how communities manage waste. We believe that with the right tools and incentives, everyone can contribute to solving the global waste crisis.

We aim to:

  • Empower citizens to report waste issues in their communities
  • Connect individuals with recycling centers to sell recyclable waste
  • Reduce food waste by facilitating food donations to those in need
  • Gamify waste management through points, badges, and community recognition
  • Provide valuable environmental impact data to local authorities

Our Story

How TrashClick came to be and our journey so far

The Idea

December 2024

TrashClick began as a simple idea during a local community cleanup. Our founders noticed that waste management was a global problem that lacked efficient technological solutions. They envisioned a platform that would make it easy for citizens to report waste, connect with recycling centers, and track their environmental impact.

Launch

upcoming

After months of development and testing, we launched our first pilot program in 5 cities. The response was overwhelming, with thousands of waste reports and hundreds of connections made between citizens and recycling centers in the first month alone.

Growth

upcoming

By the beginning of 2022, TrashClick had expanded to over 50 cities, partnered with 20+ recycling companies, and helped divert over 100,000 kg of waste from landfills. Our community grew to over 50,000 active users, all working together to make their communities cleaner.

Recognition

upcoming

TrashClick was recognized as one of the most innovative environmental startups, winning the "Green Innovation Award" and securing partnerships with major environmental organizations. This recognition helped us expand our impact globally.

Today

upcoming

Today, TrashClick operates in over 100 cities across 25 countries, with a community of more than 500,000 users. We've helped divert millions of kilograms of waste from landfills, connected thousands of food donations to those in need, and created a global movement of environmental stewards.

Our Values

The core principles that guide everything we do

Environmental Impact

We believe that every action matters when it comes to protecting our planet. We strive to make measurable, positive environmental impact through our platform and operations.

Community Empowerment

We believe in empowering communities to take action on local environmental issues. By providing tools and incentives, we help citizens become agents of positive change.

Innovation

We embrace technological innovation to solve environmental challenges. We're constantly exploring new ways to make waste management more efficient, accessible, and engaging.

Collaboration

We believe that solving environmental issues requires collaboration across sectors. We work closely with governments, businesses, nonprofits, and citizens to create holistic solutions.

Data-Driven Approach

We use data to drive decisions and measure impact. By collecting and analyzing waste management data, we can identify trends, optimize solutions, and demonstrate results.

Equity & Inclusion

We believe that everyone should have access to clean environments and waste management solutions. We design our platform to be accessible and beneficial to diverse communities worldwide.
